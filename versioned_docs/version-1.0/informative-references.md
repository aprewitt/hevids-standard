# 5. Informative References

The following cross-walk is **informative**, not normative. It is intended to help organizations already working toward NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, or EU AI Act compliance understand where HEVIDS evidence overlaps with existing obligations. It does not constitute legal advice or a certified compliance mapping, and should be independently verified by qualified counsel before being relied upon for regulatory purposes.

| HEVIDS Function | EU AI Act (informative) | NIST AI RMF (informative) | ISO/IEC 42001 (informative) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Harmony | Art. 9.2(b) — examination of risks to affected persons/groups | MAP 1.1, MAP 3.1 | Annex A.5 — AI impact assessment |
| Ethics | Art. 9.2(d), Art. 10 — data and bias provisions | MAP 5.1, MEASURE 2.3 | Clause 8.2; Annex A.5.2 |
| Veracity | Art. 13 — transparency; Art. 50 — disclosure | MEASURE 2.7; GOVERN 4.1 | Clause 8.2; Annex A.6 |
